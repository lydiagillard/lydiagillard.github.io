Banana, Berry & Oat Smoothie

Need something quick for when you're running late to class? Nothing better than a smoothie that you can chuck together in less than 5 minutes. Thats quicker than the wait time at the smoothie place on campus!

Ingredients

🥛 1½ cup reduced fat milk

🥛 2 tablespoons reduced fat Greek yoghurt

🍌 1 banana

🍓 ½ cup frozen (or fresh) mixed berries

🍚 ½ cup rolled oats

Lets make it

1. Place milk, yoghurt, banana, mixed berries and oats into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth

2. Pour smoothie into two tall glasses (Optional: top with chia seeds and coconut before serving to make it instagtram worthy)
