Coconut Curry

Easy dinner, serves 4

Ingredients

🥛 1 x 225g pkt The Spice Tailor Keralan Coconut Curry

🐔 500g chicken thigh fillets, cubed

🌱 2 cups baby spinach leaves

🍚 1 x Sunrice Basmati Rice 1kg

Lets make it

1. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add spices from the spice packet and cook until the seeds pop.

2. Cook rice as per packet instructions.

3. Add chicken and cook for 10 minutes or until browned. Add the sauce and cook for a further 3 minutes. Remove from heat, stir through the spinach and leave until slightly wilted. Spoon curry on top of rice to serve.
