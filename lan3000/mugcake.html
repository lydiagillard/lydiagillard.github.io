Mug Cake

Need something sweet without having to go to the hassle of making a big cake? Try this healthy mug cake that only uses six ingredients and will be ready in under 10 minutes.

Lets make it

1. Place the date in a medium sized heatproof bowl. Add one teaspoon boiling water and mash with a fork.

2. Add the egg, apple, oat bran and cocoa powder and stir until smooth and well combined.

3. Spoon mixture into a 160ml (2/3 cup) capacity microwave-safe mug or ramekin. Microwave on HIGH for 1 1/2 minutes or until the cake starts to come away from the sides and top is just firm. Serve dusted with extra cocoa.
