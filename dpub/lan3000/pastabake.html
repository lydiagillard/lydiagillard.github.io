Chicken Pasta Bake

This is a pasta bake that takes a little more time to make, but will impress your mum when she sees that you are capable of adulting.

Ingredients

🍗 1 roast whole chicken cooked shredded

🍝 500 g bowtie pasta shapes

🥛 300 ml thickened cream

🍗 2 chicken stock cubes

🥘 1 brown onion diced large

🥓 250 g bacon rashers sliced

🧀 1 cup cheese grated

Lets make it

1. Preheat oven to 190C

2. Cook pasta according to packet directions

3. While pasta is cooking, mix together cream, onion and bacon. Crumble both chicken stock cubes over the cream mixture and stir together

4. Drain pasta, add cream mixture and stir to combine

5. Add chicken, mix well

6. Spread into a baking tray, cover with cheese and bake for 20-30 minutes or until cheese is golden

7. Serve up and watch the smiles on your guests face as they realise they wont be eating instant noodles tonight
