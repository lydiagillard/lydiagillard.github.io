Simple Butter Chicken Pizza

Easy dinner, serves 4

Ingredients

🐔 500g Woolworths RSPCA Approved Fresh Chicken Thigh Fillets, Skinless

🍕 1 x 420g jar Sharwoods Butter Chicken Simmer Sauce

🍕 1 x 300g Toscano Classic Pizza bases

🍅 1 x punnet cherry tomatoes

Lets make it

1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 4-5 minutes each side until golden.

2. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add simmer sauce and cook for 8-10 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce is slightly reduced. Slice chicken.

3. Prepare pizza bases according to instructions.

4. Top pizza bases with chicken mixture and cherry tomatoes to serve.
